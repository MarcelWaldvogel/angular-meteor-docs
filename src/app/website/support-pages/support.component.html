Join Our Great Community!

First, head over to our community gitter channel and introduce yourself to our friendly community members.
Our community members are always happy to help answer questions and lend a helping hand to someone getting started with the framework.
Nothing is more frustrating than getting stuck at the beginning, but most questions when starting a new project can be answered quickly by posting to the community channel.
In addition to Gitter, you can also ask questions on our GitHub repository or on Stack Overflow.

If you believe that you have found a bug in Angular-Meteor itself, please post an issue to the github repository and it will be reviewed soon.
Are you looking to support open source? Our core team provides discounted rates to companies who want to fund bug fixes.
